Frequently Asked Questions

How can I play my files??

You can open your files by clicking the "Open File" option in the main menu or by dragging them into the player. Support for PDFs, videos, audio, and images is available.

Can I control playback?

Yes! Our player offers intuitive buttons to easily play, pause, fast-forward, rewind, and adjust the volume.

Does the player work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our responsive design ensures a smooth experience on smartphones and tablets.

I need installation?

No! Our player works directly in the browser, without the need to install additional software.
